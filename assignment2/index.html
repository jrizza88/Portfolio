About me

Jamar Torres was born in Boston, MA. He graduated at Northeastern University with a BA in International Affairs. Jamar has extensive experience in international issues, travel and cultural understanding. He has completed a social enterprise program in India, spent time in the education field in Turkey, completed a dialogue program geared toward diplomacy and has worked for a student exchange program. Jamar currently works at the UN and began his coding career via Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. Jamar has developed a passion for learning to code and hopes to make it a life long endeavor. He is determined to bring a combination of creativity, hardwork and his prior experiences to be the best that he can be in any venture and project that he is a part of. He will be ready for future web development opportunities beginning in November 2016.
